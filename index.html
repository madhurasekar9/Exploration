NYC Galleries
Accepting New Artists

Browse through a vast collection of galleries and museums accepting submissions and take the first step towards your artistic journey.

The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)

Accepts submissions from artists who have not yet had a solo exhibition at a major institution. Submission requirements and guidelines can be found on their website.

The Whitney Museum of American Art

Accepts submissions from emerging and established artists. Submission guidelines and application forms can be found on their website.

Guggenheim Museum

Accepts proposals from artists and curators for solo exhibitions and group shows. Submission guidelines and forms can be found on their website.

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

Accepts submissions from artists and curators for exhibitions and special projects. Submission guidelines and requirements can be found on their website.

The National Museum of Women in the Arts

Accepts submissions from female artists for their annual exhibition, Women to Watch. Submission guidelines and requirements can be found on their website.
